IAM Permissions Explorer – Product Management Assignment

Section 1: Overview 

This assignment presents a structured approach to identifying and reducing excessive IAM permissions across multi-cloud environments. The solution focuses on risk visibility, explainable scoring, and safe remediation through simulation and governance workflows.

Section 2: What’s Included

1. Problem Statement & User Persona
2. 3-Screen Wireframe (Dashboard → Identity → Simulation)
3. Feature Overview & Success Metrics

Section 3: Product Flow

Screen 1: Risk-based dashboard to prioritize identities
Screen 2: Identity-level investigation with explainable scoring
Screen 3: Safe remediation through simulation and approval

Section 4: Key Focus Areas

Risk-based prioritization
Transparent scoring model
Usage-aware remediation
Governance and audit support
